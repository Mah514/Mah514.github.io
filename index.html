Stay Safe and Healthy With AI Fitness

By Mahmoud Idlbi and Frederick Munro

It can be difficult to find the motivation to exercise and stay health; and this couldn't be any more true than in the case of the pandemic. As most cities shut down completely in an attempt to limit the spread, many of us have been spending more and more time at home. This can have serious impacts on mental and physical health; especially for the elderly. Therefore, it is perhaps more crucial than ever to exercise regularly and to stay healthy. However, it can be difficult for the elderly to find exercises that are safe and appropriate for them without the help of a personal trainer. AI can change this, our application uses advanced machine learning models to recommend exercises for everyone depending on their health status and conditions. Furthermore, the application can provide real time form-feedback using only your phone's camera.
